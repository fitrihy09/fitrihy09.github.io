Programs

Barre Basic
Recommended for barre beginners who want to give this workout a try. This class focuses on isometric holds and repetitions which are essential for building endurance.

Barre Boost
Perfect for anyone who are in need of some cardio fix! Our Barre Boost workout includes more cardio and jumping-based exercises, and focuses on core stabilisation to increase the calories burn and endurance.

Barre Burn
Add some sparks into your workout routine with our Barre Burn class. This workout combines high interval training (HIIT) and barre exercises to improve your endurance and condition your cardiovascular system.

Barre Balanced
Give your body a good pampering after an intense workout session with some invigorating stretches. Our Barre Balanced class focuses on dynamic and static stretching techniques to relieve tension around the neck, shoulders and spine. It also helps you to lengthen your muscles and strengthen your supporting core.
